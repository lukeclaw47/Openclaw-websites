About Oliver's Loom Bands

Oliver’s Loom Bands was started by Oliver Threlfo, an enthusiastic young entrepreneur who loves crafting colourful loom bands for family, friends, and local kids. Since launching his business on the Gold Coast, Oliver has brought smiles to many with his imaginative designs and personal service. Every band is handmade with care and customised to match your favourite colours or special event themes. Choosing Oliver's Loom Bands means supporting a small local business with big heart and creativity.

How It Works

  1. Get in Touch: Contact Oliver to discuss your colours, designs, and order size.
  2. Design Selection: Choose from a variety of styles or request a custom creation.
  3. Handmade Crafting: Oliver carefully makes each loom band by hand.
  4. Delivery/Pickup: Receive your colourful, personalised order ready to wear or gift.
  5. Enjoy & Share: Show off your unique loom bands or delight loved ones with thoughtful gifts.

FAQ

What colours and designs are available?
Oliver offers a wide range of bright, vivid colours and patterns. Tell Oliver your preferences, and he can also create custom combinations.
How long does it take to get my order?
Most loom band orders are completed within 3-5 business days. For larger or custom orders, Oliver will provide an estimated timeline.
Can I order in bulk for a party or school event?
Absolutely! Oliver can create bulk orders with matching or mixed designs tailored for your event. Contact him for a quote.
Is this service only for Gold Coast residents?
Primarily yes, but delivery options can be discussed for nearby areas. Collection is available locally.
